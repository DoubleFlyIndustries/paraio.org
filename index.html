Para

A general-purpose back-end framework for the cloud.

About Para

Para was designed as a simple and modular back-end framework for object persistence and retrieval. It helps your application store its objects to a datastore (SQL or NoSQL based) while automatically caching those objects and making them searchable. Why use it? Because it's open source, well documented and tested and it's just easier than most frameworks out there.

The name "Para" means "steam" in Bulgarian. And just like steam is used to power stuff, you can use Para to power your mobile or web application back-end.
